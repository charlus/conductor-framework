# Brand Voice

How do you communicate? This guides how your AI partners write copy, error messages, and user-facing text.

---

## Prompts to get started

- If your product were a person, how would they talk?
- What tone fits your users? (Casual? Professional? Playful? Direct?)
- What words do you use? What words do you avoid?
- Are there brands whose voice you admire or want to emulate?
